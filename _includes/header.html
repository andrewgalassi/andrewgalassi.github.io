I am a Ph.D. candidate in Aeronautics and Astronautics at Stanford University. My advisor is Prof. Marco Pavone, director of the Autonomous Systems Laboratory. My current research focuses on Autonomous Mobility on Demand: we build models and design control algorithms for fleets of self-driving vehicles at the city scale.

I received a M.Sc. in Space Engineering from Politecnico di Milano, a M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering from Politecnico di Torino and the Diploma from the Alta Scuola Politecnica in 2013. In 2011-2012, I spent seven months at SUPAERO as an exchange student (Admis Sur Titre). Prior to that, I received my B.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering from Politecnico di Milano in 2010.

When not in the lab, I enjoy astronomy, photography and reading.
